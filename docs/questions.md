# MACU Intelligence - Sample Questions

## Member Service Questions

### Product Information
- What are the current auto loan rates?
- What's the APY on our High-Yield Savings account?
- What are the requirements for a HELOC?
- How do I explain the benefits of our Visa Rewards Card?
- What's the minimum balance for a Share Certificate?

### Member Lookups
- How many active members do we have?
- What is the average credit score of our PLATINUM tier members?
- How many members have both a mortgage and an auto loan?
- What's our total deposit balance across all accounts?
- Show me members in St. George with more than $100,000 in deposits.

### Account Inquiries
- What's the breakdown of account types by count and balance?
- How many share certificates are maturing in the next 30 days?
- What percentage of our checking accounts have overdraft protection?
- Which account type has the highest average balance?

## Loan Operations Questions

### Portfolio Analysis
- What is our current auto loan portfolio balance?
- How many loans are 30+ days past due?
- What's the average interest rate on our mortgage loans?
- Show me loan delinquency rates by loan type.
- What percentage of our loans have autopay enrolled?

### Risk Assessment
- What is the default risk for a member with a 680 credit score requesting a $35,000 auto loan?
- Should we approve a personal loan for someone with 42% DTI ratio?
- What factors should I consider for a loan applicant who is self-employed?
- What's the recommended maximum loan amount for someone earning $75,000 with $1,500 monthly debt?

### Application Guidance
- What documents do we need for a mortgage refinance?
- What credit score is needed for our best auto loan rate?
- How long does loan approval typically take?

## Compliance Questions

### BSA/AML
- What are our SAR filing requirements?
- When do we need to file a CTR?
- What are the red flags for structuring?
- How do we handle OFAC matches?

### Regulatory
- What disclosures are required for new checking accounts?
- What are our fair lending requirements?
- How do we handle a Regulation E dispute?
- What's our privacy policy for sharing member information?

### Policies
- What's our policy on elder financial exploitation?
- When should I escalate a fraud concern?
- What are our record retention requirements?

## Support & Service Questions

### Interaction History
- Show me examples of how we handled card dispute resolutions.
- What are common reasons members call about their auto loans?
- How have we resolved mobile banking login issues?
- What's our typical response to wire transfer requests?

### Metrics
- What's our average call handle time?
- What's our first contact resolution rate?
- How does satisfaction vary by interaction type?
- Which branch has the highest satisfaction scores?

## Analytics Questions

### Member Engagement
- What percentage of members use digital banking?
- How many members are at risk of churning?
- What's our mobile app adoption rate?
- Which acquisition channel brings the most valuable members?

### Trends
- How has our membership grown over the past year?
- What's the trend in transaction volume by month?
- Are delinquency rates improving or worsening?
- Which product categories show the most growth?

### Fraud Detection
- How many transactions had high fraud scores this week?
- What are the most common fraud indicators we're seeing?
- Are international transactions showing unusual patterns?

## Complex Multi-Part Questions

- "I have a member with 720 credit score and $80,000 income who wants a $40,000 auto loan. They already have a mortgage with us. What would you recommend and what's their risk profile?"

- "A member is upset about a declined international transaction. Can you help me understand what might have caused it and how we typically resolve these situations?"

- "We're preparing for an audit. What are our BSA compliance requirements and how do we document suspicious activity?"

- "I need to explain to a new member the benefits of upgrading from Basic Checking to Rewards Checking. What should I highlight?"

- "A business member wants to wire $15,000 overseas. What compliance steps do I need to follow?"
